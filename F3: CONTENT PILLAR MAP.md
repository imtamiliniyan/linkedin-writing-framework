## Post Archetypes

| Archetype | Purpose | Structure | Pillar | How Often |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| System Reveal | Show a real build with outcome. Drives saves and DMs. | Pain confession hook → what I built → how it works (tool names and steps) → result with numbers → keyword CTA | P1 | 2x per week |
| Paradox Open | Reach new people. Drives comments and reach. | Two opposite outcomes, same situation → Why? → Destroy obvious answer → Reframe sentence → Framework with signpost → Binary question | P2 | 2x per week |
| Early Discovery | Authority and saves. New tool or integration revealed. | Tool just did X → what it now makes possible → exact setup steps → save-triggering question | P3 | 1x per week |
| Client Proof | Convert warm followers into inbound DMs. | Result number first → client before state → what I built → after state with metrics → soft DM CTA | P1 | 1x per week |
| Contrarian | Drives reposts from people who agree. New reach. | Common belief → one-line subversion → evidence from real builds → nuanced reality → debate question | P2 | 1x per week |
| Raw Observation | Fills posting days. Drives comments. | 1 to 3 lines. Something noticed this week while building or talking to clients. No teaching. No CTA. | P4 | as needed |

## Monthly Content Mix

Total posts per month: 28 to 32 (daily posting)

Pillar 1 posts: 11 to 13 (System Reveals and Client Proof)
Pillar 2 posts: 8 to 10 (Paradox Opens and Contrarians)
Pillar 3 posts: 5 to 7 (Early Discovery and Tool Comparisons)
Pillar 4 posts: 3 to 4 (Builder Journey and Raw Observations)

Additionally: 3 educational infographics per week from the Infographic Engine project covering broad AI topics for reach.

---

## Pillar Distribution Check

Before writing any batch of posts, check:
- Is at least 40% of the batch from Pillar 1 (real builds)?
- Is there at least one Client Proof post in the week?
- Is there at least one Early Discovery post using a tool update from the past 7 days?
- Are the Paradox Open hooks pulling from F2 pain points in the ICP's own words?

If any check fails, rebalance before writing.
